Here are a few reasons why HTML is a great first step:

  • It's easy to learn and understand.
  • It's the foundation of all web pages.
  • It's required knowledge for CSS and JavaScript.

Conclusion

Starting with HTML opens the door to a wide world of possibilities in web development. It's your first step toward building your own websites and web apps!
